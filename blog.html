About Us

Songiri Internationals

Songiri Internationals is a forward looking technology oriented Company having advanced manufacturing, testing and packaging facilities, along with experienced staff. The company is commited to regular up-gradation of manufacturing and testing techniques to produce quality products.

Company’s modern manufacturing faclity is located at RIICO industrial area Malpura Tonk Rajasthan.



Established in 1985, having three decades of experience and exporting Handicrafts products to USA , Europe and U.K over the year.

With our complete infrastructure to provide all types of finishes with sufficient capacity to produce quality products.

Songiri Internationals , has a unique scope of diversity in its products. This is where the old techniques meet the new designs and the fusion of both is a result that becomes the demand of today’s market.
